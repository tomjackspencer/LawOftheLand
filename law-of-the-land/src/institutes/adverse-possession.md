Adverse possession allows a person in possession of land to acquire title against the paper owner. The modern law has two regimes, and the examinable difficulty is knowing which applies.

## Two regimes

For unregistered land, and for registered land where time ran before the Land Registration Act 2002, the old limitation rules apply. For registered land under the 2002 Act, the squatter must apply to be registered, and the scheme is deliberately far less generous.

## The trap

State which regime applies before doing anything else. The two lead to different results on the same facts.
