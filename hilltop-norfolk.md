The Court of Appeal in *Hilltop Experiences v Norfolk CC* [2026] EWCA Civ 541 declined to widen the category of material considerations. The reasoning is orthodox. The interest lies in what the court refused to do.

A material consideration must bear on the use and development of land. The applicant's argument would have admitted considerations of a quite different character. The court was right to resist it.
